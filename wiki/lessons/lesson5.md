# Lesson 5: Basic Questions

Learn to ask questions in Laturo!

## Question Words

| Laturo | English | Pronunciation |
|--------|---------|---------------|
| vo | what | voh |
| vokano | who | voh-KAH-noh |
| vokimo | when | voh-KEE-moh |
| vokapo | where | voh-KAH-poh |
| vokuro | why | voh-KOO-roh |
| vokalo | how | voh-KAH-loh |
| vokanto | how much/many | voh-KAHN-toh |

## Asking "What"

```
Vo esta?
What is this?

Vo ista?
What is that?

Vo to estar?
What are you?

Vo to estar haČerelo?
What are you doing?
```

## Asking "Who"

```
Vokano to estar?
Who are you?

Vokano lo estar?
Who is he?

Vokano estar aquí?
Who is here?
```

## Asking "Where"

```
Vokapo to estar?
Where are you?

Vokapo estar libro?
Where is the book?

Vokapo mor estar irelo?
Where are we going?
```

## Asking "When"

```
Vokimo to estar llegarelo?
When are you arriving?

Vokimo estar reunionelo?
When is the meeting?
```

## Asking "Why"

```
Vokuro to estar aquí?
Why are you here?

Vokuro lo ne estar?
Why is he not here?
```

## Asking "How"

```
Vokalo to estar?
How are you?

Vokalo esta estar trabajarelo?
How does this work?
```

## Yes/No Questions

Simply use rising intonation or add "vo" at the end:

```
To estar estudiante?
Are you a student?

To estar estudiante vo?
Are you a student?
```

## Practice

Ask questions:
1. What is this?
2. Who are you?
3. Where is the book?
4. When is the meeting?
5. Why are you here?

Answer these:
1. Vokano to estar? (Your name)
2. Vokapo to estar? (here)
3. Vokalo to estar? (well)

## Answers

**Questions:**
1. Vo esta?
2. Vokano to estar?
3. Vokapo estar libro?
4. Vokimo estar reunionelo?
5. Vokuro to estar aquí?

**Answers (examples):**
1. Mo estar [Your Name].
2. Mo estar aquí.
3. Mo estar bono.

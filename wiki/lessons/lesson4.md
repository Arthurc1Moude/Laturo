# Lesson 4: The Verb "estar" (to be)

The most important verb in Laturo!

## Present Tense

| Person | Laturo | English |
|--------|--------|---------|
| mo | mo estar | I am |
| to | to estar | you are |
| lo | lo estar | he is |
| la | la estar | she is |
| le | le estar | it is |
| mor | mor estar | we are |
| tor | tor estar | you (plural) are |
| lor | lor estar | they are |

## Sentence Structure

Laturo uses SVO (Subject-Verb-Object) order:

```
Subject + Verb + Object/Adjective
Mo + estar + bono
I + am + well
```

## Common Uses

### Describing State

```
Mo estar feliz.
I am happy.

To estar triste.
You are sad.

Lo estar cansado.
He is tired.
```

### Location

```
Mo estar aquí.
I am here.

La estar allá.
She is there.

Mor estar en casa.
We are at home.
```

### Identity

```
Mo estar estudiante.
I am a student.

To estar profesor.
You are a teacher.

Esta estar libro.
This is a book.
```

## Negative Form

Add "ne" before the verb:

```
Mo ne estar feliz.
I am not happy.

To ne estar aquí.
You are not here.

Lor ne estar estudiantes.
They are not students.
```

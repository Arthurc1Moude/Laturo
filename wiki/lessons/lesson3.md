# Lesson 3: Greetings and Politeness

Learn how to greet people and be polite in Laturo!

## Basic Greetings

| Laturo | English | When |
|--------|---------|------|
| Saludelo | Hello | Anytime |
| Buenoselo diasalo | Good morning | Morning |
| Buenaselo tardesalo | Good afternoon | Afternoon |
| Buenaselo nočesalo | Good evening | Evening |
| Adioselo | Goodbye | Leaving |

## Polite Words

| Laturo | English |
|--------|---------|
| Gračaselo | Thank you |
| Mučaselo gračasalo | Thank you very much |
| Porfavorelo | Please |
| Diskulpalo | Excuse me / Sorry |
| Denadalo | You're welcome |

## Common Phrases

```
Komo estas to?
How are you?

Mo estar bono, gračaselo.
I am well, thank you.

Yelo to?
And you?

Mučelo gustoalo!
Nice to meet you!

Igualmentelo!
Likewise!
```

## Simple Conversation

```
Person A: Saludelo! Komo estas to?
          Hello! How are you?

Person B: Bono, gračaselo. Yelo to?
          Good, thank you. And you?

Person A: Mo estar bono talvo.
          I am good too.
```

## Practice

How do you say:
1. Good morning
2. Thank you
3. Please
4. Goodbye
5. How are you?

## Answers

1. Buenoselo diasalo
2. Gračaselo
3. Porfavorelo
4. Adioselo
5. Komo estas to?

## Cultural Note

**IMPORTANT:** Always capitalize person's names in Laturo. Not doing so is considered disrespectful!

✅ Correct: Kiro, Maria, John
❌ Wrong: kiro, maria, john

# Lesson 2: Numbers and Counting

Learn to count in Laturo!

## Numbers 0-10

| Number | Laturo | Pronunciation |
|--------|--------|---------------|
| 0 | nulo | NOO-loh |
| 1 | uniko | oo-NEE-koh |
| 2 | duvo | DOO-voh |
| 3 | triko | TREE-koh |
| 4 | katvo | KAHT-voh |
| 5 | penko | PEHN-koh |
| 6 | sesvo | SEHS-voh |
| 7 | septo | SEHP-toh |
| 8 | okto | OHK-toh |
| 9 | nevo | NEH-voh |
| 10 | deko | DEH-koh |

## Numbers 11-20

The pattern is simple: deko + number

| Number | Laturo |
|--------|--------|
| 11 | dekuno |
| 12 | dekduvo |
| 13 | dektriko |
| 14 | dekkatvo |
| 15 | dekpenko |
| 16 | deksesvo |
| 17 | deksepto |
| 18 | dekokto |
| 19 | deknevo |
| 20 | duvodeko |

## Larger Numbers

| Number | Laturo |
|--------|--------|

| 1000 | milo |

## Using Numbers

```
Mo estar uniko.
I am one. / I am alone.

To estar duvo amigos.
You are two friends.

Esta estar triko libros.
These are three books.

Mor estar penko estudiantes.
We are five students.
```

## Practice

Write in Laturo:
1. 7
2. 15
3. 20
4. 3
5. 12

Count from 1 to 10 out loud!

## Answers

1. septo
2. dekpenko
3. duvodeko
4. triko
5. dekduvo
